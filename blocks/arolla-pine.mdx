Arolla Pine is the signature wood type of Alpine Whispers, inspired by the Arolla Pine (_Pinus cembra_) native to the European Alps. It has a dark, warm tone with deep brown tones that suits mountain cabin and chalet builds. All Arolla Pine wood blocks share the same strength as Oak and Spruce equivalents.

## Natural blocks

These blocks generate in the world or are obtained from the Arolla Pine tree:

- **Arolla Pine Log** — standard rotatable log block
- **Arolla Pine Wood** — bark-on all-sides log
- **Stripped Arolla Pine Log** — use an axe on the log to strip
- **Stripped Arolla Pine Wood** — use an axe on the wood block to strip
- **Arolla Pine Leaves** — foliage block; can be covered with snow (see tip below)
- **Arolla Pine Sapling** — grows into a full Arolla Pine tree when planted

<Tip>
**Snowy Arolla Pine Leaves** — right-click the leaves with a Snowball to add a snow texture. Right-click the snowy leaves with an empty hand to remove the snow. This works on thrown Snowballs as well.
</Tip>

## Building blocks

Standard construction blocks crafted from Arolla Pine Planks:

| Block | Notes |
|-------|-------|
| Arolla Pine Planks | Dark, warm-toned base plank; strength 2.0 / blast resistance 3.0 |
| Arolla Pine Stairs | Standard stair shape |
| Arolla Pine Slab | Half-block slab |
| Arolla Pine Fence | Connects to adjacent fences and solid blocks |
| Arolla Pine Fence Gate | Openable fence gate |
| Arolla Pine Door | Right-click or redstone to open; uses Oak door sounds |
| Arolla Pine Trapdoor | Hinged trapdoor, placeable on any face |
| Arolla Pine Pressure Plate | Triggers from player/mob weight |
| Arolla Pine Button | Momentary redstone pulse on click |
| Arolla Pine Window | Full glass block variant; transparent, no occlusion |
| Arolla Pine Window Pane | Pane-style glass that connects to adjacent panes and blocks |

## Furniture

Alpine Whispers adds a full suite of craftable Arolla Pine furniture. Each piece is described below.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Chair" icon="chair">
    A sit-able chair block. Right-click to mount the chair and ride it as an entity. Other players can also sit.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Table" icon="table-cells">
    Connects visually to adjacent Arolla Pine Tables to form a continuous tabletop surface.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Sofa" icon="couch">
    A sit-able sofa that connects sideways to adjacent Sofa blocks. Right-click to sit. Supports multiple seated players.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Bed" icon="bed">
    A CozyBed-style block. Right-click to sleep through the night. Crafted like a standard Minecraft bed using Arolla Pine planks.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Cabinet" icon="box">
    Storage block with an inventory. Right-click to open or close; uses wooden trapdoor open/close sounds. Emits a comparator signal proportional to fill level.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Wall Cabinet" icon="box-archive">
    A ceiling-mounted, slim-profile storage variant of the Cabinet. Mounts against a wall and opens downward. Uses the same trapdoor sounds.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Cooking Aisle" icon="utensils">
    A ConnectibleCabinet that joins seamlessly to adjacent Cooking Aisle blocks for a continuous kitchen counter look. Right-click to open storage.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Dresser" icon="dresser">
    Storage block with a tabletop. Connects horizontally to adjacent Dressers. Right-click to open inventory; uses wooden trapdoor sounds.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Wardrobe" icon="shirt">
    A tall two-block-high armour storage unit. Right-click to open or close (wooden door sounds). Right-click while holding an armour piece to store it. Shift-right-click to retrieve stored armour.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Bathtub" icon="bath">
    A decorative two-block-wide bathtub with fluid rendering. Right-click with an empty hand, Water Bucket, or Glass Bottle to start filling (45 seconds). Drain with an empty Bucket (Water Bucket) or Glass Bottle (Water Potion). Removes harmful effects from entities inside when filled.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Sink" icon="droplet">
    Stone-style two-block-tall sink. Right-click with an empty hand, Water Bucket, or Glass Bottle to fill. Drain with an empty Bucket (Water Bucket) or Glass Bottle (Water Potion).
  </Card>
  <Card title="Washbasin" icon="droplet">
    Wooden-style variant of the Sink with decorative legs. Same fill/drain interactions as the Sink. Pairs well with the Bathtub for a bathroom corner.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Privy" icon="house">
    An outhouse compost block. Right-click with excess food items to grind them into Rotten Flesh, with a small chance of Bone Meal. Shift-right-click to release stored contents.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Smoke Oven" icon="fire">
    Cooks food items exactly like a vanilla Smoker — twice as fast as a Furnace. Right-click to open the cooking interface and insert food and fuel.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Note>
The Arolla Pine Smoke Oven is ideal for cooking Reindeer meat. Place Raw Reindeer in the top slot and any fuel below to produce Cooked Reindeer in half the time of a standard Furnace.
</Note>

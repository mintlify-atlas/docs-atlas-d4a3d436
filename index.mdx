Alpine Whispers is a Minecraft 1.21.1 mod that transforms snowy highlands into a richly detailed alpine world. It introduces rideable Reindeer, shearable Alpine Sheep, a complete Arolla Pine wood set with cozy furniture, Alpine Gneiss stone blocks, and a full range of winter decorations — all available on both Fabric and NeoForge.

## Get started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Install the mod">
    Download Alpine Whispers from [Modrinth](https://modrinth.com/mod/lets-do-alpine-whispers) or [CurseForge](https://www.curseforge.com/minecraft/mc-mods/lets-do-alpine-whispers) and place it in your `mods/` folder alongside Architectury API.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find a snowy biome">
    Load into a world and seek out Snowy Plains, Grove, or Snowy Slopes biomes — that's where Reindeer roam and alpine plants grow.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Harvest Arolla Pine">
    Cut down an Arolla Pine tree (the distinctive conifer in Grove biomes) to start crafting the full furniture and building set.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Build your alpine lodge">
    Combine Arolla Pine planks with Alpine Gneiss stone to construct cozy mountain chalets, lodges, or entire villages.
  </Step>
</Steps>

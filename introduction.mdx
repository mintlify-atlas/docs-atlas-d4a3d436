Alpine Whispers is the alpine chapter of the Let's Do collection. It transforms snowy highlands into a richly detailed mountain environment — introducing new wildlife, a complete Arolla Pine wood set, Alpine Gneiss stonework, and a full suite of winter decorations. The mod targets Minecraft 1.21.1 and runs on both Fabric and NeoForge.

<Note>
  Alpine Whispers requires **Architectury API** as a dependency. The game will not start without it. See the [installation guide](/installation) for details.
</Note>

Alpine Whispers supports both Fabric and NeoForge for Minecraft 1.21.1.

  <Tab title="Fabric">

## The Let's Do collection

Alpine Whispers is part of the [Let's Do collection](https://lets-do.ch/mods/) — a family of Minecraft mods that each expand a distinct theme or environment. Every mod in the collection is built to complement the others, so you can install one or combine several for a cohesive experience. The collection is maintained by the same team and follows shared design principles: new content that fits naturally into the vanilla game.

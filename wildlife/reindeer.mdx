Reindeer are the flagship creature of Alpine Whispers. These large, tame-able ungulates roam snowy mountain biomes in small herds, can carry two riders simultaneously, and will defend themselves when threatened. Taming one opens up a fast and atmospheric way to traverse the alpine landscape.

## Spawning

Reindeer spawn naturally in snowy biomes, including the **Grove**, **Snowy Plains**, and **Snowy Slopes**. They appear in small groups and can often be spotted grazing or moving together across open snowfields.

## Taming

Reindeer are tamed by feeding them **Sweet Berries**. Hold Sweet Berries in your hand and approach slowly — the reindeer will be drawn toward you via a tempt behaviour before you can feed it. Continue feeding until hearts appear and the reindeer is tamed.

<Note>
Sweet Berries are also used for **breeding** tamed reindeer. Feed two tamed adults to put them in love mode and produce a calf.
</Note>

## Riding

Once tamed and fully grown, a reindeer can be ridden without a saddle — it is considered "saddled" automatically when tamed and adult. Right-click an adult tamed reindeer (without sneaking) to mount it.

**Two players can ride the same reindeer at once.** The first passenger sits toward the front; the second sits toward the back. The reindeer accepts a new passenger as long as fewer than two entities are already riding it.

The rider in the front seat controls movement using standard WASD/look inputs.

<Tip>
Reindeer can carry two players — great for exploring snowy mountains together without splitting up.
</Tip>

## Herd behaviour

Adult reindeer use a custom `ReindeerHerdGoal` that causes them to stay loosely grouped. An adult reindeer will move toward the nearest other adult reindeer it detects within **18 blocks**, stopping once it closes to within **4 blocks**. This goal is suppressed when a reindeer is being ridden or is still a baby, so mounted reindeer will not break formation to chase the herd.

| Parameter | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Detection radius | 18 blocks |
| Minimum following distance | 4 blocks |

## Combat & survival

<Warning>
Wild reindeer will retaliate if attacked. The `HurtByTargetGoal` is set to alert others, meaning nearby reindeer may join in defending an injured companion. Approach wild herds carefully.
</Warning>

Reindeer are wary of **Wolves** (flee at 14 blocks) and **Polar Bears** (flee at 12 blocks). When cornered or harmed, they fight back using `AnimationAttackGoal` — a melee attack that plays a full attack animation before dealing damage, with an attack delay of 21 ticks.

- **Max Health:** 40 HP
- **Attack Damage:** 7
- **Attack Knockback:** 1.6

## Movement on snow and ice

Reindeer have adapted locomotion on alpine terrain:

- **Snow, powder snow, and snow blocks:** movement speed is multiplied by **1.125** (a ~12.5% boost), making reindeer noticeably faster on their native ground.
- **Ice, packed ice, frosted ice, and blue ice:** horizontal momentum is dampened to **60%** on each ground tick, causing the reindeer to slide and lose speed.

This means reindeer are fastest on fresh snow and slowest when crossing ice sheets.

## Drops

- **Raw Reindeer** — dropped on death under normal circumstances.
- **Cooked Reindeer** — dropped if the reindeer dies while on fire.

Cooked Reindeer can also be crafted into a **Cooked Reindeer Dish** using the Arolla Pine Smoker.
